OSRS Combat Achievements — route planner

Find the easiest tasks you haven't done and map a route to the next tier. Data: live OSRS Wiki crowdsourced completion rates.
Sync your account via WikiSync — enable the WikiSync RuneLite plugin and log in once so your data is uploaded. For the live lookup, run the app with python3 serve.py (opening the file directly blocks the network call).
Other ways to import (no WikiSync)
Paste a RuneLite Tasks Tracker export (JSON), or just enter your current CA points.

Completion % = share of players (with any CA done) who have completed that task — the wiki's crowdsourced difficulty signal. Ease score blends completion % (45%), tier (35%) and task type (20%). Progress is saved in your browser. Not affiliated with Jagex. Data © OSRS Wiki contributors.
